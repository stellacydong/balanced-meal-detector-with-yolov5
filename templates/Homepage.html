Mental Health & 
Eating

Mental health is tightly woven with eating. The nutrients we give to our bodies can regulate our mood. 

Sometimes controlling eating can become a hoping mechanism for people; however, this is an extremely dangerous way to cope with stressors. Counting calories, dieting, and "clean" eating can become obsessions that alter brain function and put our bodies into a state of fight or flight starvation thinking. 

If you are struggling with overthinking about food and body image it is important to reach out for help. Mental health is just important as the physical health of the rest of our body.
